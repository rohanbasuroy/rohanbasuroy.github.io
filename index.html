Rohan Basu Roy
রোহন বসু রায়

Welcome to my webpage. I am a Ph.D. candidate at Northeastern University, Boston. I work with Professor Devesh Tiwari. I perform research on scheduling and optimizations in Cloud Computing and HPC, especially focussing on Serverless Computing. 

Apart from my laboratory, you will find me hanging out in concerts, listening or playing heavy metal music, and trying new food items.

Contact me at: basuroy.r@northeastern.edu
